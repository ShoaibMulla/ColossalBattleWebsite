A Unique PVP Battle Arena Experience

Colossal Battle is a fantasy-based battle arena game where 2 teams of 3 players each fight against each other in the Land of Valorna using combat strategy and skills to prove their valor.

Colossal Battle gives a unique gameplay experience to the players. Players can:

  • Choose their favorite hero from many, each with a unique perk and ultimate combat move
  • Assign different battle roles to their hero before each battle
  • Choose and customize awesome combat and special skills for their hero for the battle
  • Choose fierce creatures to transform into during battles and spread havoc in the battlefield

We are a small passionate team working on Colossal Battle and want to make it the best playing experience for the players.
